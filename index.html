Overwatch



Developed by Blizzard Entertainment released in may of 2016. Available on the following platforms:
Microsoft Windows, Xbox One, Playstation 4, and Nintendo Switch

What is the main objective?

Players are placed in a 6v6 scenario, typically with 2 players on each class: Tank, Damage, and Support. Each role has a different style kit and plays a critical role in contributing to the outcome of the game.

Counterplay is an important aspect in Overwatch, and as you climb into higher skill tiers teamwork becomes more of a critical role in winning the game. Each hero has a different skill floor and skill ceiling. A hero which would be easy to pick up such as Reaper, but has a lower skill ceiling. This hero loses effectiveness as you go higher in skill rating but is easier to obtain value from at low skill levels. A hero with a low skill floor but high skill ceiling would be genji, hanzo, or doomfist.
